Unit 2 수일치, 능동/수동, 시제

S질량의 보존의 법칙
단수주어+단수동사   |   복수주어+복수동사
명사는 "-s"가 있으면 "복수" / 동사는 "-s"가 있으면 "단수"
          |  단수주어  |  복수주어  |
          |   A boy   |    Boys    |
  종류    |  현재시제  |  현재시제   |  과거시제
 be 동사  |     is    |    are     |  was / were
 do 동사  |   does    |     do     |     did
have 동사 |    has    |    have    |     had
일반동사  |    plays  |    play    |   played

[ 주어 + 거품 + 동사 ]

1. 주어 + 부사 + 동사
John usually (arrive / arrives) early for meetings.
John은 3인칭 단수이기 때문에 arrives
Connor Industries frequently (update / updates) its software systems.
Connor Industries는 교유명사다. 이유는 두 단어 앞에 대문자이기 때문이다.
그러므로 단수 취급해서 updates

2. 주어 + 전치사구 + 동사
The employees at the branch office (analyze / analyzes) customer feedback every month.
The employees 주어에 -s 있으니까 analyze이다. at the branch office -> 전치사구
The students in the laboratory (have / has) conducted experiments for 6 months.
The students 주어에 -s 있으니까 have이다. in the laboratory -> 전치사구

3. 주어 + 분사구 + 동사
The seminar held last month (have / has) been acclaimed for its insightful presentation.
The seminar 주어에 -s 없으니까 has이다. held last month -> 분사구
The participants attending the job fair (was / were) actively engaged in group discussions.
The participants 주어에 -s 있으니까 were이다. attending the job fair -> 분사구

4. 주어 + 관계절 + 동사
The skills I could learn at the class (was / were) invaluable.
The skills 주어에 -s 있으니까 were이다. I could learn at the class -> 관계대명사절
The newly hired CEO, who has worked for more than a decade, (play / plays) an important role in the company's strategic planning.
The newly hired CEO 주어에 -s 없으니까 plays이다. who has worked for more than a decade -> 관계대명사절

Those who + 복수동사
Anyone who + 단수동사

[ 무조건 동사원형 자리 ]

1. 조동사 + 동사원형
As of next month, Mr.Cameron will (oversee / oversees) the upcoming project.
조동사 will, can, should, may 등등 뒤에 동사원형 oversee
The budget report will (be / is) submitted by the end of this week.
조동사 will, can, should, may 등등 뒤에 동사원형 be

2. Please + 동사원형
Please (confirm / confirms / confirmed) your attendance for conference by Friday.
confirm
Please (review / reviews / reviewed) the presentation slides before the meeting tomorrow.
review

[ 무조건 단수동사 ]

주어자리에 단수명사, 불가산명사, To부정사구, 동명사구, That절 이면 단수동사를 써야 한다.
The new smartphone model (is / are) available in white and black.  단수명사 smartphone model -> is
All equiopment (have / has) been thoroughly inspected and tested.  불가산명사 All equiopment -> has
To follow the company's policies (are / is) essential.  To부정사 To follow the company's policies 여기서 policies가 -s라고 해서 복수명사로 끝났어도 is
Sharing regular updates (enhance / enhances) communication within the team.  Sharing regular updates 여기서 updates가 -s라고 해서 복수명사로 끝났어도 enhance
That the company's profits exceeded expectations (was / were) surprising.  That절 was
